Project Intro

  Player’s Advantage is the next platform for all Fortnite players to analyze their own stats as well as their opponents. This platform will house the stats of millions of players to help improve skills to gain an edge over one’s opponents. Stay up to date with your performance in the game and measure your rate of improvement using our statistics straight from the Fortnite API.

  Our team at Player’s Advantage strive to give the player access to information otherwise hidden within the Fortnite interface. We want to serve as a one-stop shop for all users looking to analyze their gameplay. Through the use of our product, the Fortnite player-base could track their improvement and have all their stats stored in one place.

We have identified the following stats that whoudl be tracked based on our research:

Career Stats

  • Number of Total Wins
  • Win percentage over all game modes
  • Number of Total Kills
  • Kill/Death Ratio

Solo Gamemode Stats

  • Number of Solo Gamemode Wins
  • Win percentage within solo gamemode
  • Number of Top 10 finishes in solo
  • Number of Top 25 finishes in solo
  • Number of Kills in solo
  • Kill/Death Ratio in solo
  • Average kills per match in solo

Duo Gamemode Stats

  • Number of Duo Gamemode Wins
  • Win percentage within duo gamemode
  • Number of Top 5 finishes in duo
  • Number of Top 12 finishes in duo
  • Number of kills in duo
  • Kill/Death Ratio in duo
  • Average kills per match in duo

Duo Gamemode Stats

  • Number of Squad Gamemode Wins
  • Win percentage within squad gamemode
  • Number of Top 3 finishes in squad
  • Number of Top 6 finishes in squad
  • Number of kills in squad
  • Kill/Death Ratio in squad
  • Average kills per match in squad

Recent Match History

  A match history would be provided to allow the player to monitor their performance in their most recent gaming sessions. This feature allows the player to reflect on how they played to in turn improve their future games. A big selling point of statistic websites are showing off to friends and fellow gamers. Match histories are perfect for recording highlight games to share afterwards.

The match history will bring similar info to light as highlighted above, for example:

  • Gamemode played
  • Number of kills

Use Cases

  Bragging to one’s friends or peers is a large part in gaming culture nowadays. The competitive spirit within us all, desires to be better than those around us. Many games lack an effective way of sharing one’s achievements. Through publicizing these statistics, players have access to all their achievements. The internet is readily available in modern society, so we are able to streamline the sharing of player statistics.

  With the emergence of esports culture and the rise of competition within the gaming environment, statistics are a good representation of a gamer’s skill. Competition within the scene is at an all time high with the many success stories that spawned from humble beginnings. For example, the growth of Twitch Streamer “Ninja” shows how skill within a game could translate to a large fanbase and mountain high viewership. Such huge successes in the industry are spawning more and more gamers who wish to strive for the same degree of success within their chosen videogame. We are here to provide a tool for these people to help them improve and display their skills through thenumbers and not only through word of mouth. Player statistics could be one’s ticket to recognition and a separation from the large crowd of people who make up the casual player base. Our service is their resume and their gateway to improvement, as they reflect on their past achievements and build upon their foundations.

  We at Player’s Advantage are here for the people, but we also desire to aid the developers of the game and bring improvements to what is lacking. We look to publicize otherwise hidden statistics to bring attention to the problems within the game. Balancing issues plague the competitive gaming industry due to the nature of the practice and the almost zero chance of perfecting the art. Through analyzing the numbers, balancing faults could be found and possibly eased with game patches in the future.

Stakeholders
